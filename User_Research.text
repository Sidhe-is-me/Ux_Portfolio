Speak with a minimum of 5 users to find out-
Is there a need for this system?
If not, how can you change it so that it meets a need?
Wh are the main user groups?
What day-to-day activities do they engage in that is related to the product?
What is the workflow(the sequence of activities)?

Make sure you actually observe them, not just interview them.
Don't over think, just get out and speak to some users.
